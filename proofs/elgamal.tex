Decryption of El-Gamal works since the following can be said:
\begin{align*}
  y &= y_2(y_1^a)^{-1}\\
  y_2(y_1^a)^{-1} &= m\beta^k(\alpha^{ak})^{1} \mod p\\
  &= m\alpha^{ak}(\alpha^{ak})^{-1} \mod p\\
  &= m \mod p
\end{align*}
